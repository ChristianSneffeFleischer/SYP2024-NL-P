Crystal Palace have finalised the signing of French midfielder Naouirou Ahamada from Stuttgart. The 20-year-old, who spent time with the Juventus academy before his move to Germany, has put pen to paper on a three-and-a-half year contract at Selhurst Park.    I   m very happy to have joined this club. It is an amazing club, and I   m very excited to get started," said Ahamada after completing his move.    I want to show my quality, and I want to help this team with my quality. I want to play games, work hard, train hard and progress, and help the team.    Chairman Steve Parish added:    We are delighted to welcome Naouirou to Crystal Palace.
