Pedro Porro has claimed that he missed Sporting CP training on Monday as he was determined to complete a late window move to Tottenham Hotspur. 90min first revealed in December that Spurs had made contact with Sporting over a potential January transfer for Porro, but were told they would need to pay his    45m release clause up front. After a long month of negotiations, Tottenham have now agreed to pay    48m in several instalments. Porro, who was initially expected to have a medical in London on Monday, had to wait to travel to England as talks spilled over into this week. He was granted permission to miss Sporting training, and speaking to reporters at Lisbon airport late on Monday night, reaffirmed his love for the club despite the imminent move.
