Atletico Madrid have held talks with Adama Traore over a free transfer at the end of the season, 90min understands. Traore's contract with Wolves is set to expire in the summer and terms are unlikely to be agreed over a new deal. The winger is enjoying life at Molineux under compatriot Julen Lopetegui but it is increasingly likely that he will depart Wolves. 90min reported on Tuesday that Atletico are stepping up their efforts to sign Caglar Soyuncu from Leicester on a Bosman deal, and they are now also exploring a free transfer for Traore.
