Shakhtar Donetsk winger Mykhaylo Mudryk has confessed he "can't say no" if he gets the chance to join Arsenal, who are interested in signing him. After impressing with 10 assists and two goals for Shakhtarlast season, Mudryk was linked with a move to North London towards the end of this summer's transfer window. Such a move never went through, but Arsenal are reported to still be interested and could make an approach for the winger in January.  Speaking to CBS Sports, the 21-year-old, who has been dubbed 'the Ukrainian Neymar', admitted he wouldn't be able to turn down the chance to join the English side, but is remaining focused on his current club until January comes around. "I do dream (of the Premier League). I think every guy dreams about the Premier League," he said.
