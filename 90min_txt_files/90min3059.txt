Manchester City have dismissed talk that a deal is place to sell Bernardo Silva to Barcelona this summer, 90min has been told. Sources close to City have told 90min that there is no deal or agreement for Silva and that reports insisting otherwise are wide of the mark. It has been reported in Spain that Barcelona, who are long-term admirers of Silva, have a deal in place to take the Portugal international to Camp Nou. It was claimed that Bar  a, Man City, Silva and his agent Jorge Mendes were all in agreement about a move that would see him replace Frenkie de Jong at the club upon his departure to Man Utd. But City have dismissed this as speculation and are believed to have no plans to let Silva go.
