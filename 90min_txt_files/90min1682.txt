Bournemouth are close to agreeing a fee with Sassuolo for Hamed Traore, sources have told 90min. The 22-year-old has returned to form in the Neroverdi starting XI after recovering from a foot injury earlier this season, and notably picked up an assist in this weekend's shock 5-2 win at champions AC Milan. Traore, the brother of Manchester United starlet Amad Diallo, is now the subject of interest from Bournemouth, and 90min understands the Cherries are close to agreeing a deal to sign the Ivory Coast international.
