RB Leipzig manager Marco Rose has insisted that centre-back Josko Gvardiol is not for sale this summer. The 21-year-old was targeted by Chelsea last summer and the Blues remain keen in 2023, while the likes of Liverpool, Manchester City and Real Madrid are known to be admirers of the Croatian World Cup star. Recently, Gvardiol confirmed his desire to play in the Premier League in the future and even specifically confessed he would like to continue working with French forward Christopher Nkunku, who has agreed to join Chelsea at the end of the season. His comments have reignited the speculation surrounding him but Rose has warned Gvardiol's suitors that the defender will be going nowhere. "Josko Gvardiol will be a RB Leipzig player next season," Rose said. "He is happy here. "He said he wants to play in the Premier League but he didn   t say when.
