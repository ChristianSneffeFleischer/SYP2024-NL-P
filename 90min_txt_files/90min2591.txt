Southampton are the latest club to make an approach for PSV Eindhoven star Cody Gakpo, 90min understands. The Netherlands international has attracted interest from Manchester United, while Nottingham Forest had a bid rejected earlier this summer. Fellow Premier League sides Everton, Leeds, Leicester and Newcastle have also been keeping tabs on his situation. Sources have told 90min Southampton have spoken to PSV about Gakpo and made it clear they would be willing to pay up to    40m, but the Eindhoven giants are understood to be looking for    50m. 90min also understands Gakpo is unclear about his future and considering his options with the World Cup now just months away.
