Tottenham Hotspur have officially confirmed the loan signing of wide player Arnaut Danjuma until the end of the season from Villarreal. The 25-year-old was widely expected to join Everton earlier in the month but Tottenham swooped in to highjack the deal in recent days. A club statement on the signing reads: "We are delighted to announce the signing of Arnaut Danjuma on loan from Villarreal  until the end of the 2022/23 season, subject to international clearance. "Born in Lagos, Nigeria, Arnaut began his career as a youngster in the Netherlands with TOP Oss and PSV before joining NEC Nijmegen in 2016.
