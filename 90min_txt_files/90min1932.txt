Aston Villa have submitted a  £10m bid to sign Real Betis left-back Alex Moreno, sources have told 90min. Unai Emery is on the hunt for squad reinforcements in his first transfer window as the club's head coach, with their need for new recruits underlined by Sunday's shock 2-1 loss at home to Stevenage in the FA Cup. 90min understands that Villa were one of several clubs keeping an eye on Benfica's Alex Grimaldo as his contract in Lisbon winds down, but they have instead made an immediate move to try and bring Moreno to the West Midlands.
