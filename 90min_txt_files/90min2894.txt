Chelsea have confirmed the permanent signing of talented American goalkeeper Gabriel Slonina from MLS team Chicago Fire.  The 18-year-old has put pen to paper on a six-year deal and will spend the remainder of the year on loan at Fire. 90min reported a few days ago that the two clubs agreed a fee for the goalkeeper, who had previously been of interest to the likes of Real Madrid in recent months. The fee is believed to be worth around $15m - a record transfer for an American goalkeeper.
