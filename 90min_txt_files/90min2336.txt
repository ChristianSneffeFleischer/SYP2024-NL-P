(DAZN) Liverpool have joined Chelsea and Real Madrid in the race to sign Bruno Guimaraes from Newcastle United, though the midfielder is happy on Tyneside and is likely to be offered a new contract to fend off suitors. (TNT Sports) Arsenal will reignite their interest in Shakhtar Donetsk winger Mykhaylo Mudryk and Villarreal forward Yeremy Pino in the January window as Mikel Arteta looks to bolster his forward options. (The Sun) Inter believe that centre-back Milan Skriniar will sign a new contract and stay at the club beyond the end of this season despite links with Manchester City, PSG and Tottenham.
