Fulham have made a bid to sign Fluminense midfielder Andre, 90min understands. The 21-year-old has quickly established himself as one of Fluminense's top players and a number of sides across Europe have expressed an interest in luring him away from his homeland in 2023. Sources have confirmed to 90min that Fulham have approached Fluminense to try and thrash out a deal, but the Brazilian side are reluctant to part ways with their star player during the 2023 campaign. Andre, who has been linked with the likes of Barcelona and Liverpool, has spoken out about the interest and insisted his focus remains solely on his current employers. "Obviously, I'm focused and very happy," Andre said recently. "I just renewed my contract until 2026. "It's normal to get offers, but I'm focused here on Fluminense and having an excellent season."
