The 2023 January transfer window offered clubs throughout Europe the chance to bolster their squads for the second half of the season. There was no shortage of cash spent in the immediate aftermath of the 2022 World Cup, as clubs at the top and bottom of the table sought to address the issues to have plagued during the first half of the campaign. Chelsea were the window's biggest spenders, while lowly Everton failed to make a single signing despite their relegation woes.
