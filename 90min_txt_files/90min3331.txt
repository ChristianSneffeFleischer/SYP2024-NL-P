Manchester United may launch a surprise bid for Chelsea midfielder Mason Mount, although the Blues intend on holding talks over a new contract. Manchester City and Liverpool are also interested in signing 23-year-old Mount should Chelsea be willing to consider a sale. Real Madrid are closing in on the signing of Monaco midfielder Aurelien Tchouameni. The 22-year-old agreed terms weeks ago, alongside Liverpool and Chelsea, but his intention has always been to head to the Santiago Bernabeu. Barcelona manager Xavi has spoken with Wolves midfielder Ruben Neves over a potential summer transfer. Villarreal winger Arnaut Danjuma has spoken of Liverpool's previous interest in signing him and says he would always consider moving to a higher level. He has a  £45m release clause in his Yellow Submarines contract. Manchester City utility star Oleksandr Zinchenko is on the radar of both Everton and Newcastle. The Ukrainian is capable of playing at both left-back and in central midfield. Raheem Sterling is another Manchester City player who could be leaving the Etihad Stadium. Chelsea, Barcelona, Real Madrid and Bayern Munich are all eyeing the 27-year-old who has just one-year left on his contract. Paris Saint-Germain full-back Achraf Hakimi is a target for Chelsea, who are able to buy players again after Todd Boehly's takeover ended the sanctions against the club. Frenkie de Jong says he's aware of Manchester United's interest in him, but would prefer to stay at Barcelona this summer if given the choice. Chelsea have revived their interest in Barcelona's American full-back Sergino Dest, who is only two years into the five-year contract he signed at Camp Nou in 2020. Newcastle are ready to launch a bid for Everton striker Dominic Calvert-Lewin, with the Toffees under pressure to balance their books to comply with Financial Fair Play (FFP) regulations. Juventus are ready to move for Manchester City centre-back Aymeric Laporte as they bid to strengthen their squad ahead of a Serie A title push. Corentin Tolisso, recently released by Bayern Munich after five years at the club, is being tracked by Manchester United and Arsenal.
