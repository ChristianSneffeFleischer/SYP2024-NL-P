Aston Villa are interested in signing Matteo Guendouzi from Marseille this transfer window, 90min understands. Guendouzi previously worked under Villa boss Unai Emery for a year and a half at former club Arsenal between 2018 and 2019. Sources have told 90min that the Spanish coach is interested in reuniting with the Frenchman at Villa Park this month. However, Guendouzi is believed to be happy at the Stade Velodrome, while Marseille value the midfielder at around    50m and they are not actively looking to move him on.
