David de Gea has revealed that he wants to stay at Manchester United beyond this season, though was unable to provide an update on contract talks with the club. The Spaniard's deal expires at the end of the current campaign, and while United retain an option to extend it by a further year, they have yet to activate it. 90min understands that De Gea, whose inability to play from the back under new manager Erik ten Hag has been called into question, is wanted by former club Atletico Madrid as a potential replacement for Jan Oblak. But speaking after United's 2-1 win at Everton on Sunday evening, De Gea insisted that he wants to stay at Old Trafford for many more years.
