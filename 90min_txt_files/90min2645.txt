West Ham United are pushing to agree a deal worth in excess of    50m including bonuses for Lucas Paqueta, having seen an initial bid of    40m turned down by Lyon, sources have told 90min. The Hammers are rooted to the bottom of the Premier League table after defeats in their opening three league games, and are actively looking to increase the quality in David Moyes' squad despite making seven summer signings to date. 90min reported on Wednesday that West Ham had made a bid for 24-year-old Paqueta, who has also been of interest to Arsenal and Newcastle, worth    40m - the value they understood from intermediaries that Lyon were looking for. However, sources confirmed to 90min that the offer had been turned down by Lyon, to the surprise of West Ham.
